Temper Trap

The site was intended to be an information source for fans to catch up on latest news and events from the band.

It was decided the best way to get this functionality quickly and within a restricted time scale was to use drupal CMS. As drupal is very flexible it suited the quite challenging functionality concepts whilst being easy fairly straight forward to adapt visually.
