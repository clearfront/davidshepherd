LWFG

The NHS needed the site to not just be eye-catching but also be quick loading and engaging for the user.

The challenge for this project was that the site needed to be created using an existing CMS system.

My role involved working with the designer to achieve a workable design that was also interesting and unique for the user.

  • Year: 2011
  • Role(s): UI Designer and Developer
  • Agency / Client: Mccann Erikson

Challenges

Uneven and unique design

The look of the page was not a straight forward array of square boxes. The content elements presented quite challenge in the sense that the content areas were not regularly shaped and figuring out an approach that could be possible with such a unique design took some trial and error.

But in the end a solution was found and some compromises had to be accepted.

Design

Generally the design was intended to be eye-catching and unique and in this instance it succeeded. Whilst the design threw up a number of issues when it came to constructing it the finished site was well received and felt satisfying as a finish project.

Technology

Jquery and Jquery UI were used to enhance and add interactive features to the site.

The site had to be put on to an existing umbraco CMS.

Outcome

The final results felt by all was a success. The intended eye-catching and unique design was achieve whilst getting past all the issue threw up by the design.
